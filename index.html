This genetic algorithm that implements DNA to evolves flies over generations passing the skills of the fittest to the following generation. The flies learn to reach the target by evolution.
The existing GAs are founded upon the following main principles:

  • Reproduction
  • Fitness
  • Crossover
  • Mutation
