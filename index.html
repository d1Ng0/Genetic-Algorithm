This genetic algorithms teaches implements DNA which evolves over generations passing the information of the fittest to the following geneations so the flies learn to reach the target.
The existing GAs are founded upon the following main principles:

  • Reproduction
  • Fitness
  • Crossover
  • Mutation

Have a play at creating obstacles (by click and drag with the mouse pointer) and see how the flies evolve and eventually find a path. My work and study is based on ShiftMan and his awesome learning material "Nature of Code"
